  • Real Name: Jimmy Donaldson (more commonly know as Mr. Beast)
  • Birthday and Birthplace: May 7, 1998 and Kansas, United States
  • Age and Sign: 22 years old and Taurus
  • Occupation: American Youtuber known for his philanthropy, excessive use of money, and immense support for PewDiePie

Mr. Beast (Jimmy Donaldson) may be considered as Generation Z's biggest philanthropist, as he has engaged in several activities to better mankind and the world. One of his biggest projects is "Team Trees," where he raised over $21 million to plant over 20,000,000 trees. On top of that, he spends a huge amount of money every single video, in which most goes to helping underprivilidged and the rest to feed the needs of our entertainment.
